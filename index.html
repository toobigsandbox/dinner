The Rhythm

Weekly Schedule

The consistent 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM structure. Predictability reduces anxiety and builds confidence.

5:00 - 5:15

Arrival & Regulate

Magnets, Name Tags, Aprons, and Mood Meter check-ins.

5:15 - 5:30

The Walkthrough

Sensory warnings (fans, heat), tour of the active zones, and safety checks.

5:30 - 7:00

Skills Lab (90 Min)

The core block. Knives, Heat, Muddling, and Assembly. Watch → Try → Own loop.

7:00 - 7:15

The Recharge

Mandatory clean-up, 20-second handwash, and an Earth Zone breathing break.

7:15 - 7:45

Family Meal

Eating together, practicing the service scripts, and sharing one "delight".

7:45 - 8:00

The Reset

Transition Station. Earning stickers, Reflection Cards, and Skills Passport.
