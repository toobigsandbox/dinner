The Rhythm

The Daily Schedule

The consistent 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM (3-hour) structure. Stretching the timeline reduces anxiety, removes rushing, and guarantees time for reflection.

5:00 - 5:15 PM

Arrival & Regulate

Magnets, Name Tags, Aprons, and Mood Meter check-ins at the Cooldown Cart.

5:15 - 5:30 PM

The Walkthrough

Sensory warnings (fans, heat), tour of the active zones, and safety checks.

5:30 - 7:00 PM

Skills Lab (90 Min)

The core block. Knives, Heat, Muddling, and Assembly. Heavy focus on the Watch → Try → Own loop.

7:00 - 7:15 PM

The Recharge

Mandatory clean-up, 20-second handwash, and an Earth Zone breathing break before service.

7:15 - 7:45 PM

Family Meal

Eating together, practicing the service scripts, and sharing one "delight" from the day.

7:45 - 8:00 PM

The Reset

Transition Station. Earning stickers, completing Reflection Cards, and updating the Skills Passport.
